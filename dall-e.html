DALL·E

On the 27th of June 2022, I was invited to use the DALL·E image generation AI, and have created many images with it since, posting most on my Instagram account. The AI is honestly astonishing sometimes- producing images which look like photos or accurately recreating real art styles in simply a few senconds - and is otherwise still really impressive. My favourite examples are all below, and if you hover over them the prompt I used to generate that image will appear.
